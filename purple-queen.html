A message for you, before the day arrives

The Purple Queen
of My Sky Blue Heart

Happy Birthday, in advance

Read on

Before your birthday arrives and the world rushes in with its noise and celebration, I wanted to reach you first, quietly, in this small and honest space, just to say — I see you.

I see The Purple Queen of My Sky Blue Heart, and I need you to know what that means to me. Purple, because you carry a royalty that was never handed to you — you built it from within. It is in the way you carry your spirit, the way you love deeply even when love asks the most of you. You are rare in the way that only things of true worth ever are.

And sky blue is mine — the part of me that belongs to you. You colour it. Without you it is just air; with you it is something worth looking up at. My heart has a hue because of you, and that hue is the calmest, most beautiful thing I know.

I know we have not been in an easy season. I know there are words we are still learning how to say, distances we are still learning how to close. But I want you to hear this clearly: rough patches are not the end of the story. They are the part where we find out how much we mean to each other. And you mean everything to me.

You are not just valuable to me — you are irreplaceable. There is no version of any good thing in my life that does not have you somewhere at the centre of it. Your smile is not something I take for granted. Your happiness is not something I am indifferent to. I want you smiling. I want you knowing, on this birthday and every day before and after it, that you are cherished beyond what words are really capable of holding.

So before the candles, before the cake, before the calendar turns — this is for you, from me. Happy birthday, my Queen. May this season bring you everything you deserve: warmth, softness, joy, and the certainty that you are deeply, completely loved.

The Purple Queen of My Sky Blue Heart

Always yours — with love that does not waver
